Home / Departments/Department of Communication and Mass Media/Ram Media

Expand your skills through one of our many on-campus student-run media outlets! Being part of Ram Media allows you to:

  • Help broadcast weekly shows and Angelo State sports.
  • Produce and/or host radio programs.
  • Cover campus news, sports and lifestyles for our digital newspaper.

Ram Media Outlets

Ram TV

Prepare for careers in the media industry by training as reporters, videographers and editors.

Ram TV Sports

Gain hands-on experience in sports broadcasting as part of the team that broadcasts Angelo State athletic events.

Ram Radio

Be one of the voices for the Rams! You could even produce your own radio show.

Ram Page

Join our student-run digital newspaper that contains current campus and community news, as well as features, sports, editorials, cartoons and more.
