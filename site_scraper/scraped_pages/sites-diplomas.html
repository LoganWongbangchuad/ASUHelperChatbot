Diplomas

Diploma Name

Your legal name will appear on your diploma.

Mailing of Diploma

  • Your diploma will be mailed approximately six to eight weeks after commencement, providing there are no financial encumbrances and all requirements have been met.
  • It is essential that you provide a correct mailing address on the Application for Graduation for your diploma to be mailed to you.
  • Participation in commencement does not guarantee graduation or receipt of a diploma.
  • If you are an international student who will no longer be in the country after graduation, we highly recommend having your diploma sent by FedEx. Please contact the Center for International Studies at cis@angelo.edu to make arrangements.

Diploma Frames

Diploma frames are available in the ASU Bookstore.
