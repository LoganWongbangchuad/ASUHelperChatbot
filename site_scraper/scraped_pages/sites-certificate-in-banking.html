About the Program

  • Courses can be completed in person, totally online or in a hybrid in-person/online format.
  • Enhance your career opportunities by gaining the skills critical to successful commercial banking professionals, including credit analysts, commercial lenders and loan officers.
  • Our program includes a paid internship. You can also benefit from special programs like “Meet the Banks” with our local banking partners.

Quick Links

Department of Accounting, Economics and Finance

The Norris-Vincent College of Business is accredited by AACSB International.

Competition in the banking industry for knowledgeable professionals is very intense, with ability and experience currently in high demand. Our curriculum is designed in collaboration with our banking partners to provide you with training previously available only through industry programs.

Credit Requirements

This graduate-level banking certificate program consists of 12 credit hours. That means you only have to complete four courses to earn your certificate.

Application Deadlines

  • Spring A: Dec 15
  • Spring B: Applications will open the day after the first class day of the regular term and 1st 8-week session*
  • Spring B: Mar 4
  • Summer: May 20
  • Fall A: Aug 1
  • Fall B: Applications will open the day after the first class day of the regular term and 1st 8-week session*
  • Fall B: Oct 1

*Please see the Academic Calendar for B session application availability.

Certificate Courses (3 credit hours each)

  • FINC 6361: Financial Institution Administration*
  • FINC 6324: Credit Analysis and Lending
  • FINC 6371: Internship in Finance
  • MKTG 6323: Sales*

Total credit hours: 12

* These courses have a prerequisite of FINC 6301.
