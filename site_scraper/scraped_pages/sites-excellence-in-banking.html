Excellence in Banking

The Excellence in Banking Program (EIB) provides a Certification in Banking for Business majors or a Minor in Banking for AgriBusiness & other majors upon graduation.

In addition to your degree from Angelo State University, these enhance your opportunities by providing skills critical to successful commercial banking professionals.

Competition in the banking industry for knowledgeable bankers is very intense, with ability and experience currently in high demand. The need for quality trained bankers is reflected in the compensation seen throughout the industry.

Our curriculum was specifically designed by the NVCOB, in collaboration with our banking partners, to provide students with training previously available only through industry programs. The courses are designed to complement a business or agribusiness degree, and many of the courses can be utilized to meet either existing course or elective requirements.

Students who participate in this program are required to complete a banking internship. The internships are competitive and paid.

Certificate in Banking

15 Credit Hours

  • Accounting 3311 - Financial Accounting and Reporting I
  • Finance 4324 - Credit Analysis and Lending
  • Finance 4361 – Financial Institution Administration
  • Finance 4371 – Internship
  • Marketing 3323 - Sales

Prerequisites must be met for all courses selected in this certificate.

Minor in Banking for Non-Business Majors

18 Credit Hours

  • Accounting 2301 – Principles of Accounting
  • Accounting 2302 – Managerial Accounting
  • Finance 3361 – Financial Management
  • Finance 4324 – Credit Analysis and Lending
  • Finance 4371 – Internship
  • Marketing 3323 – Sales

Prerequisites must be met for all courses selected in this minor. This minor is not available to students with an undergraduate major in business.
