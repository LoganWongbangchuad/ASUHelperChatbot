Around the Department

Kinesiology and exercise science play a vital role in the health and wellness of individuals and our society. Your ASU degree is your ticket to join this dynamic and growing industry that helps keep us fit and healthy, coaches and advises athletes, provides opportunities for recreation and play, and rehabs us when we are injured.

Take a look at some other aspects of our department that make ASU an outstanding place to study kinesiology.

Fitness Facilities

Take advantage of our state-of-the-art Ben Kelly Center for Human Performance. It houses an extensive weight room, fitness machines, rock-climbing wall, gymnasium, indoor track, racquetball courts, indoor pool and locker rooms.

High-Tech Labs

Get the hands-on experience you need in our modern labs equipped with medical-grade equipment, like an EKG machine, metabolic cart, ergometers, biological impedance instrument and hologic DEXA X-ray machine.

Professional Experience

We will help you set up internships and shadowing opportunities with allied health professionals at various fitness, wellness and recreation organizations. You can also join our department’s student organizations to learn leadership skills, take part in social and community activities, and network with students and professionals.
