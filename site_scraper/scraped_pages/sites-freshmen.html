Your Future, Our Commitment.

Our scholarships are funded by private donors, businesses and organizations that have joined ASU in our commitment to your academic success.

ASU Distinguished Scholarships

Our premier scholarships are awarded based on your academic achievement, and you don’t even have to apply. You are automatically considered once you are admitted to ASU.

General Scholarships

Submit one application to be considered for over 350 different scholarships.

Degree-Specific Scholarships

We offer over 250 scholarships that are tied to specific degree programs.

You can apply for both our general scholarships and degree-specific scholarships with the same application.

Some scholarships require the FAFSA, so we encourage you to submit your FAFSA every year.
