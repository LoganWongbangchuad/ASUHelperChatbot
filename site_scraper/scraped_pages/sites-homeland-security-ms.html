Gain in-depth knowledge about regional, national and international security issues.

In our nationally recognized online homeland security degree program, you will delve into a comprehensive range of topics, including everything from terrorism and counter-terrorism, federal immigration laws and international trafficking - to biological and radiological hazards and responses.

You will also join a global community of like-minded students while you interact with professors who are acknowledged experts in their fields. Plus, since this degree is offered completely online, you can access our award-winning program from anywhere in the world.

What Can You Do With a Homeland Security Degree?

The increasing complexity of national security issues is driving an ongoing demand for homeland security professionals and a steady growth in border and homeland security and emergency management jobs.

With your ASU homeland security degree, you will be prepared to start or advance your career in the military, emergency management or local, state and federal law enforcement.

Why Earn My Homeland Security Master’s Degree at Angelo State?

National Recognition

Our program is annually ranked among the best and most affordable in the nation by multiple online higher education guides, including Intelligent.com, PublicServiceDegrees.com and many others.

Emergency Management Certificate

You can earn an Emergency Management Certificate by completing a specific four-course sequence within your degree plan. That means you can get a valuable added credential to help advance your career without having to take extra classes.

Elite Partnerships

We have existing Articulation Agreements with various universities, military branches and federal agencies, including the National Security Agency and National Cryptologic University, which allow you to receive academic credit for prior training related to intelligence and homeland security.
