Meet My Counselor

Our admissions counselors are here to help make your application process to Angelo State as easy and stress-free as possible.

Admissions counselors are here to help students who have questions about the undergraduate admissions process at ASU. If you are interested in pursuing a master’s degree or doctoral degree, please contact the College of Graduate Studies. If you are an international student, please contact the Center for International Studies.
