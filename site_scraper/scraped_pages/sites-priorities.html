Campaign Priorities

“Leading the Charge” honors the legacy of Angelo State University while driving momentum towards the next chapter in our story. Building upon a century of success, Angelo State is uniting students, alumni, faculty, staff, friends and the community in relentless pursuit of vision, determined to make a lasting impact.

Join us as we invest in Angelo State’s future, focusing on four key areas:

Rams on the Rise

Guiding Student Success with Expanded Resources and Endowments

Angelo State’s commitment to student success is exemplified by the financial aid and scholarship opportunities across a broad range of initiatives, offering students an affordable path on their journey as Rams. Beyond financial help, we provide excellent student-life programs to support mental and physical wellbeing, ensuring a successful and enjoyable college experience.

Shepherding Success

Creating the Environment for an Enhanced Learning Experience

Raising up a new generation of Rams means continuing to invest in a cutting-edge learning experience through capital improvement projects. From broadening research and technological capabilities to expanding athletics facilities, Angelo State’s commitment to growth will be evident to anyone who visits campus.

Scaling New Heights

Advancing Angelo State’s Position as a Leader in Research and Innovation

Research at Angelo State will create tremendous opportunities for faculty and students alike, attracting grants and new academic endowment possibilities. With increased funding across all colleges, students will have more opportunities to engage in collaborative research, develop new skills and enhance their career prospects.

Stronger Together

Engaging with Community Partners for Mutual Benefit

The legacy of Angelo State is inseparable from the community it was born into. ASU enriches community life with events and supports economic growth by partnering with local organizations to create career-development opportunities that benefit the entire region.
