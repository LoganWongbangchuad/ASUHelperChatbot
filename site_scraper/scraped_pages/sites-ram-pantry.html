Distance Learner Food Pantries

To assist ASU students who are not on campus, we have put together a list of recommended resources where ASU students who face food insecurity may find community resources.

All food pantries/food banks have a website for more information about distributions schedules, location, and contact information. We advise you to call first before visiting the location to get their most updated information and to request eligibility information.

Click on the drop-down menus to find a food pantry/bank near you:

Food Bank of West Central Texas

Address: 5505 N 1st St, Abilene, TX 79603

The Food Bank of Abilene, Inc. The Food Bank of West Central Texas is a non-profit 501 (C) 3 community service organization that collects donated food to distribute to other non-profit agencies serving the needy of the Big Country.

United Methodist Service Center & Food Pantry

Address: 2618 N 1st St, Abilene, TX 79603

We currently operate two programs: The Food Pantry and the Service Center. The mission of the Food Pantry is to provide a week’s worth of nutritional groceries once every calendar month for economically disadvantaged families in Taylor County.

Christian Ministries - Highland Church of Christ

Address: 701 Walnut Street Abilene, TX 79601

Provides a food pantry. Documentation Required: Valid ID and Social Security Card(s)

Andrews Food Bank

Address: 711 NE 5th St, Andrews, TX 79714

At the West Texas Food Bank, our mission is to feed the hungry. If you need food assistance, you can start by requesting information about the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP).

Texas Health and Human Services

Address: 2017 Hutchins Ave, Ballinger, TX 76821

HHS offers help with food, health care and cash assistance for Texans. Find out if you are eligible by applying through yourtexasbenefits.com. Whether it’s Medicaid, SNAP food benefits or Temporary Assistance for Needy Families cash assistance, HHS is helping Texas families get back on their feet.

Rust Street Ministries

Address: 803 Rust St. San Angelo, TX 76903

Rust Street Ministries is a community outreach ministry located in San Angelo, Texas. We provide help with basic needs and life skills at no charge to those living in the Concho Valley area. Our programs are designed to serve the emotional and physical needs of our community by providing temporary assistance with food, clothing, household items and furniture.

First Baptist Church

Address: 1103 17th St, Brady, TX 76825

The Food Pantry distributes food to people in need on the second Friday morning of each month from 9:00-12:00. On food distribution mornings, ministry team members handle the paperwork, pray with the recipients individually, bag and box the food, and carry it to the recipients’ vehicle. In a typical month we serve over 100 families, with each family receiving around 30 lbs of food, plus an extra smaller box if there are children in the home.

Ram Pantry - Angelo State

Address: 1910 Rosemont Dr San Angelo, TX 76901

Angelo State University promotes healthy living by providing immediate food needs for its students on campus. We serve as an extension of the ministry of participating congregations exclusively for charitable purposes. Days and hours of operation are Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays from 1pm until 5pm.

Papa’s Food Pantry of Somervell County

Address: 753 East Gibbs Boulevard, Glen Rose, Texas 76043

Clients must establish proof of residency by showing their current utility bill or rent receipt and a picture ID. Food assistance is available to only one party at a given address.
