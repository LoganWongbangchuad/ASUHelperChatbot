Help

City Markers indicate cities represented in the current list.

  • Select a map marker to filter the list by that city.
  • Selected markers are shown in green.
  • Click multiple markers to add them to the selection.
  • Click selected markers again to remove them from the selection.

Select Radius

  • Click anywhere on the map to select pins within a 50 mile radius.
  • Click and drag the side handles to resize the selection.
  • Click and drag the center handle to move the selection.
  • Click pins inside the radius to remove them from the selection.
  • Click pins outside the radius to add them to the selection.
  • Click the blue shaded area to remove the selection.

     Tags

  • Selected cities appear in tags above the search field.
  • Click a tag to remove that city from the selection.
