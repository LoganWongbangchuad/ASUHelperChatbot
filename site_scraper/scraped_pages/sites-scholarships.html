Degree-Specific Scholarships

We offer over 250 scholarships that are tied to various degree programs to new and enrolled students.

Unless otherwise stated in the scholarship description, students apply for the scholarships listed below by completing the scholarship applications, especially the ASU General Scholarship Application, found on RamPort.

How to Apply

The application opens Oct. 1 with a priority date of Jan. 15.

  1. Log in to RamPort.
  2. Click on the Paying for College > Financial Aid & Scholarships page.
  3. Click “Apply for ASU Scholarships.”

Contact the departments on this page for more details about degree-specific scholarships.
