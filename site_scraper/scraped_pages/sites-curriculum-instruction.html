Department of Curriculum and Instruction

Our department is dedicated to training you for educational leadership while also allowing you to fit going to college into your busy schedule. To that end, all of our degree programs and certification plans are offered totally online on an eight-by-five schedule: eight-week blocks, five times per year.

Programs

Administrative Leadership M.A.
  • Master of Arts (M.A.) in Administrative Leadership
  • Online
The online administrative leadership master’s degree develops your administration and management skills to prepare you for organizational leadership.
Curriculum and Instruction M.A.
  • Master of Arts (M.A.) in Curriculum and Instruction - Advanced Instructor
  • Master of Arts (M.A.) in Curriculum and Instruction - Professional Education
  • Master of Arts (M.A.) in Curriculum and Instruction - Teacher Studies
  • Online
Our nationally-ranked curriculum and instruction program will teach you the necessary skills to develop engaging curriculum, design innovative instruction, coach learner development, evaluate program impact and promote professional development.
Educational Leadership M.S.
  • Master of Science (M.S.) in Educational Leadership with Texas Principal Certification
  • Online
The online master’s degree in educational leadership includes Texas principal certification and will prepare you for careers in K-12 leadership.
Mental Health and Wellness Counseling M.A.
  • Master of Arts (M.A.) in Mental Health and Wellness Counseling
  • Online
This degree will prepare you to be an ethical mental health and wellness counselor in your chosen field. It does not lead to licensure or certification, but it will set you on a track for broad integration of mental health and wellness counseling concepts in your field.
Professional Counseling M.A.
  • Master of Arts (M.A.) in Professional Counseling
  • Online
Prepare for your career as a licensed professional counselor by earning your master’s degree in professional counseling 100% online at Angelo State.
School Counseling M.Ed.
  • Master of Education (M.Ed.) in School Counseling
  • Online
The online master’s in school counseling (M.Ed. in School Counseling) will prepare you for a career as a Texas school counselor.
Student Development and Leadership in Higher Education M.Ed.
  • Master of Education (M.Ed.) in Student Development and Leadership in Higher Education
  • Online
We offer an online master of education degree that will prepare you for higher education leadership positions in non-academic arenas of student life.
Transformative Leadership Ed.D.
  • Doctor of Education (Ed.D) in Transformative Leadership
  • Online
By earning your Ed.D. in Transformative Leadership, you can become a leader in your organization to impact and inspire change.

More Options

Academic Advising Certificate
The online Academic Advising Certificate will prepare you to start or advance your career as an academic advisor on college or university campuses.

Mental Health and Wellness Counseling – Online LPC Bridge
Prepare for licensure as a licensed professional counselor (LPC) in Texas.

School Counseling – Online LPC Bridge
Prepare for licensure as a licensed professional counselor (LPC) in Texas.

Texas Principal Certification
The online Texas Principal Certification program is aligned with Texas principal standards and prepares you for leadership in K-12 education.

Texas School Counselor Certification
The online Texas School Counselor Certification program prepares qualified applicants to take the TExES exam to become a school counselor in Texas.

Texas Superintendent Certification
The online Texas Superintendent Certification program prepares qualified applicants for the TExES exam to be a superintendent of schools in Texas.

Texas Teacher Certification
The online Texas Teacher Certification program will prepare you to take the TExES exam to become a middle school or high school teacher in Texas.

Around the Department

Educating the next generation of students is an important and rewarding career choice. We applaud you for your dedication, and we are here to help you advance in your chosen field of education. We also understand that you are incredibly busy, so we offer flexible online degree programs to help you achieve your educational goals within your schedule and budget.

Take a look at some other aspects of our department that make ASU an outstanding place to study curriculum and instruction.

Flexible Online Classes

All of our course content is available online through the Blackboard software program. You can get homework assignments and complete readings, as well as interact with your classmates and professors.

Open Educational Resources (OERs)

All of our degree programs and classes utilize OERs to ease the strain on your budget. OERs are course materials that are available at no charge (FREE!) for teaching, learning and research.

Tech Savvy

You also have access to our new Innovative Teaching Center, a classroom outfitted with the same state-of-the-art technology and equipment that teachers and administrators are likely to use when they start their careers.
