This screening tool is intended for use by ASU visitors only. ASU students and employees must use the screening tool found in RamPort.

You have been cleared to come to campus.

Please print or screenshot this page to present when you arrive.

You have indicated that you have been experiencing symptoms related to COVID-19 or have been exposed to a confirmed or suspected case.

Please do the following:

  1. In order to keep the Ram Fam healthy, please do not come to campus.

  2. Look for emergency warning signs* of COVID-19.

    If you are showing any of these signs, seek emergency medical care immediately.

    • Trouble breathing
    • Persistent pain or pressure in the chest
    • New confusion
    • Inability to wake or stay awake
    • Bluish lips or face

    *This list is not all possible symptoms. Please call your medical provider for any other symptoms that are severe or concerning to you.

    Call 911 or call ahead to your local emergency facility: Notify the operator that you are seeking care as you have displayed symptoms associated with COVID-19.

  3. Contact a healthcare provider

    If you do not have the emergency warning signs, contact a healthcare provider for guidance on additional COVID-19 testing or appropriate healthcare options.

  4. Follow your healthcare provider’s recommendations

  5. Rest and take care

    If you are sick, please stay at home.
