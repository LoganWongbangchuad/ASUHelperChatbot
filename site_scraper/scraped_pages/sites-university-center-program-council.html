If you enjoy planning events that range from concerts to Christmas Tree Lightings, UCPC is the organization for you!

UCPC is one of the most active organizations on campus and brings more than 50 events and programs to campus each year, all of which are led by students. Our most notable events include:

  • Club Cafe
  • Crossroads Live
  • Springfest
  • Ram Jam concerts
  • Drag Rave
